Documents Overview

________

Detail your services

  1. Commercial invoice: A document that details the contents of a shipment, including the price and quantity of each item.
  2. Packing list: A document that lists the contents of a shipment, including the weight and dimensions of each item.
  3. Air waybill (AWB): A document that serves as a contract between the shipper and the airline, outlining the terms of transport and providing details on the shipment.
  4. Certificate of origin: A document that verifies the origin of the goods being shipped and is required by some countries to determine duty fees.
  5. Dangerous goods declaration form: A document that provides information about any hazardous materials being shipped, including their classification and proper handling procedures.
  6. Safety data sheet (SDS): A document that provides detailed information about hazardous materials, including their properties, handling procedures, and safety precautions.
  7. Packaging certificate: A document that certifies that the packaging used to transport the goods meets the necessary safety requirements.
  8. Approval from relevant authorities: A document that certifies that the shipment has been approved by the necessary authorities for transport.
  9. Temperature control record: A document that records the temperature at which a shipment was transported, often required for temperature-sensitive goods.
  10. Phytosanitary certificate: A document that certifies that plants or plant products being shipped meet the necessary health requirements.
  11. Pharmaceutical license: A document that certifies that the pharmaceuticals being shipped meet the necessary regulatory requirements.
  12. Certificate of analysis: A document that provides information about the quality and composition of a product.
  13. Health certificate: A document that certifies that an animal being shipped meets the necessary health requirements.
  14. Import permit: A document that authorizes the importation of goods into a country.
  15. Animal welfare declaration form: A document that outlines the welfare conditions of animals being transported.
  16. Vaccination records: A document that provides information about the vaccination status of an animal being transported.
  17. Transport and handling plan: A document that outlines the procedures for transporting and handling a shipment.
  18. Special permits and licenses: Documents that provide authorization for the transport of goods that require special handling or regulatory compliance.
  19. Insurance certificate: A document that verifies that the shipment is insured.
  20. Security declaration form: A document that provides information about the security of the shipment.
  21. Customs declaration form: A document that provides information about the goods being shipped for customs clearance.
  22. Proof of payment: A document that verifies that the shipper has paid for the goods being transported.
  23. Vehicle registration certificate: A document that certifies the ownership and registration of the vehicle being transported.
  24. Bill of lading: A document that serves as a contract between the shipper and the carrier, outlining the terms of transport and providing details on the shipment (primarily for ocean freight).
